Nickolas Nikolic is an editorial photographer and photojournalist from Milwaukee, WI. He studied at the University of Wisconsin – Milwaukee, where he received his Bachelor's in the Fine Arts in 2004. He has been published internationally in over 20 news-sources as of 2011. Some of his publications include ThirdCoastDigest.com, Marxist.com, and TreeHugger.com.

He believes in the potential of all individuals and looks intently for that potential in every person he meets. Social issues he finds extremely interesting.
