The Unseen Milwaukee Project

  • The Hungry

  • Objective

    • Represent Milwaukee homeless on a scale not commonly associated with them by placing them in the mass media. This is a project to raise awareness of growing homelessness in Milwaukee, and growing need for food aid in the community. Ultimately, this projects goal is to generate awareness and funds for food aid at The Gathering.

  • Method:

    • Photograph homeless on-location at The Gathering and place their portrait on a billboard in the Milwaukee area, as well as to create content for a book that will feature Milwaukee's homeless front and center.

The fundraising page is here.

The purpose of the Unseen Milwaukee project is to winnow through the details of life to discover those people that have slipped through the cracks of the mainstream.

Once I find them, I would like to place them front and center in the mainstream media where they never thought they would be. This project is to give a feeling of importance to those members of the community that never experience it, and to do so on a scale that makes those persons phenomenal.

The Unseen Milwaukee Project is also to give back to the community. Every installment of projects benefits a group working in the community. In this case, The Gathering, a community soup-kitchen that serves nearly 100,000 people a year, will receive promotion through the advertizing medium selected and proceeds of the following book.
